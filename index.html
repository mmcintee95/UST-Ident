UST-ID: The Future of Student Identification

Solving student and university ID challenges with innovation

The Problem: Student Challenges

- Lost/Stolen ID cards cause inconvenience and fees

- Locked out of dorms, dining, and events

- Physical ID cards are fragile and outdated

The Problem: University Challenges

- Managing lost/stolen cards is costly

- Security risks from lost IDs

- Physical ID production is outdated & costly

How UST-ID Works

- Securely stores student IDs in a digital format

- Uses NFC/RFID for tap-to-access functionality

- Allows instant locking and unlocking of ID
